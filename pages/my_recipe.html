Traditional Cheeseburger

A burger with beef patty and a slice of cheese

Ingredients

  • Beef Patty
  • Cheese (commonly cheddar, American, Swiss, or blue cheese)
  • Bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion (raw or sautéed)
  • Pickles (dill pickle slices)
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Bacon
  • Additional Toppings (optional, such as mushrooms, jalapeños, avocado, or fried egg)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Beef Patty
  2. Toast the Bun
  3. Prepare the Toppings
  4. Assemble the Cheeseburger
  5. Top with the Second Bun Half
